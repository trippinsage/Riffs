Our Heritage

Since 1939, Riff's Department Store has been a Newfoundland & Labrador retail staple, growing from its first Windsor (now Grand Falls-Windsor) location to 20 stores. We offer quality clothing, home goods, and more at affordable prices, with an unwavering commitment to community and customers.
