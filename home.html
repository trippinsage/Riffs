Our Heritage

Established in 1939, Riff's Department Store has been a cornerstone of Newfoundland & Labrador's retail landscape for over 86 years. From our first store in Windsor (now Grand Falls-Windsor), we’ve grown to 20 locations, offering quality clothing, home goods, and more at affordable prices. Our commitment to community and customers remains unwavering.
