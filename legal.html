Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 6, 2025

At Riff's Department Store, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website, engage with our services, or participate in our programs.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address provided voluntarily during newsletter sign-ups or customer service inquiries.
  • Usage Data: Information about your website interactions, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent, collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Location Data: Approximate location from your IP address or precise location if you enable location services for features like the store locator.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Enhance your shopping experience and process transactions.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve site functionality, personalize content, and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Trusted service providers for payment processing, website hosting, or marketing.
  • Legal authorities when required by law.
  • Third parties during business transactions like mergers or acquisitions.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures like encryption and access controls to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You may:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Disable cookies via browser settings.

Contact us at info@riffs.ca to exercise these rights.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined or as required by law.

Our website may link to external sites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.
