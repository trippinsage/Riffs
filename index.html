Our Story Since 1939

For over 85 years, Riff’s has been more than a store — we’ve been part of the fabric of Newfoundland & Labrador life. Family-owned, community-first, and proudly NL.

1939 — The Beginning

A small general store opened in Windsor, Newfoundland — the start of a legacy built on trust and value.

1970s–1990s — Growth

As communities grew, so did Riff’s — opening new stores from coast to coast across the province.

Today — 20 Stores Strong

Still 100% family-owned. Still committed to quality, value, and the people of NL.
